Club Membership

As laid forth in the Clemson Running Club Constitution, all members must pay annual dues in order to maintain membership with the organization. This is required by Clemson University Student Organizations.If you are a racing member you do not have to pay dues Dues breakdown is as follows:

$25 General Club Due - The general due covers club clothing, access to monthly dinners and team social events, and club daily runs. Payment of these dues helps to fund clothing and food for special events.

$50 Fall Racing Fee - The Fall Racing Fee must be paid for members who wish to compete in club races in the Fall semester. The racing fee covers a racing singlet, as well as all registration, travel, lodging, and gas for any fall racing event. It does not cover food while on trips.

$50 Spring Racing Fee - The Spring Racing Fee must be paid for members who wish to compete in club races in the Spring semester. The racing fee covers a racing singlet, as well as all registration, travel, lodging, and gas for any fall racing event. It does not cover food while on trips. You may pay the spring racing fee in the spring regardless of your fall racing status, or may pay it in the fall in addition to the fall racing fee.

Examples:
Non-racing member: $25
Racing member, fall only: $50
Racing member, spring only: $50
Racing member, entire year: $100

While there is no hard deadline for dues payment, we ask that all members pay as promptly as possible once they know the club is something they are interested in being involved with. Members who want their T-Shirt size garunteed must submit club dues prior to October 1st. Members who wish to race must pay dues prior to their first away race. The first home race will be free of charge to any new members interested in racing. If you have any questions regarding dues, please reach out to our President, Sophie Wichlac, at run@g.clemson.edu or swichla@g.clemson.edu.
